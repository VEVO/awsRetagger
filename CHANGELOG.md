## [Unreleased]
### Changed

## [0.0.1] - 2017-11-12
### Added
- Ability to retag AWS resources
- Supported resources:
  * CloudFront Distributions
  * CloudWatch LogGroups
  * EC2 Instances
  * ElasticSearch Domains
  * RDS Instances
  * RDS Clusters
